Welcome to Singapore Erhu Society

传承二胡艺术 · 弘扬华乐文化

Preserving Erhu Heritage · Promoting Chinese Music Culture

自1995年成立以来，我们致力于推广二胡艺术，为本地二胡爱好者提供交流与学习的平台。

Since 1995, we have been dedicated to promoting the art of Erhu and providing a platform for local Erhu enthusiasts to connect and learn.

✨ 圆满举行 Successfully Held

《琴韵三十载 · 薪火传新声》二胡学会30周年音乐会圆满落幕

30th Anniversary Concert Successfully Held

🗓️ 2025年12月21日（星期日）晚

21 December 2025 (Sunday) Evening

📍 SOTA Concert Hall

感谢所有到场嘉宾、演奏者和工作人员！三十年的风雨兼程，三十年的弦音不辍。我们见证了这个特殊时刻，聆听了来自本地、马来西亚、日本和中国近六十位演奏员的精彩演出。

Thank you to all attendees, performers and staff! We celebrated this milestone with performances from nearly 60 musicians from Singapore, Malaysia, Japan and China, showcasing the vibrant Erhu art in our multicultural society.
